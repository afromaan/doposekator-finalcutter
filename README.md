## Overview
FinalCutter is a DIY RC lawn mower with skid steering and welded frame. Our plan is to make fully functional, open-source and not too expensive and hard to make at home RC mower.

Our **first goal** is to make it work, at least. So it can drive, cut and somehow work for us.

And the **second goal** is to customizate it to its best configuration possible (still with our plans on mind). 

<br>  

<strong>That means:</strong>
1) Air Suspension 
2) Controlling Via Camera From Our Living Room
3) Autonomous Mode (with nVidia its maybe early future for us too?)

## Features
- Gasoline engine for cutting and charging
- 4 wheel drive motors
- FlySky remote control
- Raspberry Pi - motors controller
- Battery charged via alternator
